Networking in college shouldn't feel impossible

Meet students and alumni who actually want to help. Schedule real conversations. Build relationships that lead to referrals, mentors, and opportunities.

Career fairs feel like speed dating with recruiters who forget your name. LinkedIn messages to alumni go unanswered. You know networking matters, but nobody teaches you how.

Coffee Chat is different. We match you with students in your classes, alumni who recently broke into your target industry, and mentors who actually want to help — then make it easy to have real conversations.

The Story Behind Coffee Chat

S

Coffee Chat was built by a 20-year-old college student who was tired of the traditional networking grind. Stiff handshakes at career fairs, cold LinkedIn messages that go nowhere, and events where everyone's just exchanging business cards they'll never look at again.

I wanted something different — a way to build genuine relationships with people who share my curiosity, not just my industry. A place where a 30-minute coffee conversation could turn into a mentorship, a friendship, or even a co-founder match.

So I built Coffee Chat — a more human, more efficient way to network. No fluff. Just real people, real conversations, and real connections.

— Sal, Founder
