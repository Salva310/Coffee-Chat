Career fairs feel like speed dating with recruiters who forget your name. LinkedIn messages to alumni go unanswered. You know networking matters, but nobody teaches you how.

First Sip is different. We match you with fellow Rowan students, alumni who recently broke into your target industry, and mentors who actually want to help — then make it easy to have real conversations.

The Story Behind First Sip

First Sip was built by a Rowan junior who was tired of the traditional networking grind. Stiff handshakes at career fairs, cold LinkedIn messages that go nowhere, and events where everyone's just exchanging business cards they'll never look at again.

I wanted something different — a way to build genuine relationships with people who share my curiosity, not just my industry. A place where a 30-minute coffee conversation could turn into a mentorship, a friendship, or even a co-founder match.

So I built First Sip — a more human, more efficient way to network. No fluff. Just real people, real conversations, and real connections.

— Sal Altadonna, Founder

What Makes Us Different

Built for students who want real connections, not LinkedIn noise

Smart Matching

Matched based on what you're studying, your career interests, and mentorship goals. We pair you with students and alumni who align with where you're headed — not just who you already know.

Built-In Icebreakers

Every chat comes with tailored conversation starters based on your shared interests and goals. No more awkward "so what do you do?" — jump straight into topics you both care about.

Community Groups

Join groups for your major, career interest, or campus. Share advice, ask questions, and find your people in a space that feels like a group chat, not a career fair.

15–30 Minute Chats

Fits between classes. First Sip is built around quick, focused conversations that work with your schedule — whether virtual or at the campus café.
